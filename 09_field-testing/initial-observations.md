# Initial Field Testing Observations

Early testing focused on basic connectivity and signal behavior.

---

## Observations

### Elevation Impact

Raising a node even a small amount (e.g., ~10 ft) resulted in:

* noticeable increase in signal strength
* improved stability

---

### Obstructions

Buildings and terrain significantly reduced:

* signal strength
* link reliability

---

### Signal Drop-Off

Signal quality degraded quickly once line of sight was lost.

---

## Key Insight

Mesh networking performance is highly dependent on:

* line of sight
* node placement
* elevation
