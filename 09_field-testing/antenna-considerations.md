# Antenna Considerations

Wireless network performance is heavily influenced by antenna configuration.

---

## Key Factors

### Antenna Gain

Higher gain antennas:

* increase range
* reduce beam width

---

### Cable Loss

Long RF cables introduce signal loss.

Example cable types:

* RG58 → high loss
* RG316 → moderate loss
* LMR-240 → low loss (recommended for longer runs)

---

## Elevation

Increasing antenna height improves:

* line of sight
* signal propagation

Example:

* ground level vs 10ft mast can significantly increase range

---

## Practical Insight

Short cable + higher antenna placement is generally better than long cable + low placement.

---

## Relevance

These factors are critical for:

* TOC node setup
* relay node effectiveness
* long-range link stability
