# WiFi HaLow Overview

WiFi HaLow is a wireless networking technology based on the IEEE 802.11ah standard.

Official WiFi Alliance information:

https://www.wi-fi.org/discover-wi-fi/wi-fi-halow

---

## Key Characteristics

Compared to traditional WiFi standards, HaLow offers:

* longer communication range
* lower power consumption
* operation in sub-GHz spectrum

These characteristics make it attractive for IoT and long-range networking applications.

---

## Potential Applications

HaLow networks may support:

* sensor networks
* rural connectivity
* distributed monitoring systems

Because it supports IP networking, HaLow may also enable **long-range mesh networking architectures**.
