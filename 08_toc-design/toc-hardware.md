# TOC Hardware Platforms

A Tactical Operations Center (TOC) node may require more computing power than operator devices.

Possible hardware platforms include:

* laptops
* tablets
* compact servers

---

## Microsoft Surface Devices

One potential option is the
Microsoft Surface

https://www.microsoft.com/surface

Advantages:

* portable form factor
* integrated display
* sufficient processing power
* Windows compatibility with TAK ecosystem tools
