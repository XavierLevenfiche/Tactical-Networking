# TOC Deployment Strategy

Physical deployment significantly impacts network performance.

---

## Elevation Strategy

Mounting antennas higher improves:

* line of sight
* signal reach

Example:

* 10ft mast improves coverage vs ground level

---

## Mobility

TOC may be:

* static (fixed position)
* mobile (vehicle-mounted)

---

## Considerations

* stability (guy wires)
* setup time
* power availability

---

## Goal

Maximize coverage while maintaining rapid deployment capability.
