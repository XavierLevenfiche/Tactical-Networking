# Relay Node Placement Strategy

Relay nodes play a critical role in extending the effective range of the network.

---

## Purpose

Relay nodes:

* forward traffic between operator nodes and TOC
* extend communication beyond direct line-of-sight
* improve network resilience

---

## Placement Principles

### Line of Sight

Relay nodes should maintain:

* visibility to both upstream and downstream nodes

Obstructions significantly degrade performance.

---

### Elevation

Raising relay nodes improves:

* signal propagation
* coverage area

Example:

* mounting on a mast or elevated terrain

---

### Distance Balancing

Relay nodes should be placed:

* far enough to extend coverage
* close enough to maintain strong signal links

---

## Tradeoffs

| Factor          | Impact             |
| --------------- | ------------------ |
| Too few relays  | coverage gaps      |
| Too many relays | network congestion |

---

## Relevance

Effective relay placement is one of the most important factors in:

* network reliability
* range extension
* performance consistency
